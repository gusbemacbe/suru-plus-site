Upgraded Suru++ 25 Dark!

I have just upgraded the old Suru++ Dark from Suru++ 25, stopped since ! Yes, stopped for a long time, because I have focused on Suru++ 25 mainly, firstly and before all, so I could end all bugs that the original author of Suru++ Ubuntu failed to fix due to his fact of not being a developer or system analyst, after I could redesign the icons theme, to make it compatible with KDE, with new colours palette and new grid rules, based on Sam Hewitt's design guidelines and complete the icons theme.

The biggest update of Suru++... From 20 to 25!

I have stopped this development since because I was extremely on my college and my thesis. I was very busy with writing a complicated crazy and long thesis, writing in LaTeX, compiling via XeLaTeX, using the extension LaTeX Workshop on VSCode Insiders, delivering my thesis until November 28. I introduced my thesis to my thesis advisor and to three invited professors on December 8.

Finally I am graduated! 👨‍🎓 🎉

I was really on holidays, but I could not return this job, because I had to rewrite at the request of my thesis advisor and three invited professors, delivering the DVD-RW until December 14. Just after December 15, I could return this job, but I could not release it, because firstly I had to wipe up everything to terminate the bugs, redesign and complete the icons theme. Redesigning four thousand seven hundred took two months to complete the icons theme with extinction of all bugs.

Finally I have just released today... To know more what is news in the new version, you can check Changelog.
