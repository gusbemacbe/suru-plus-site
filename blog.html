Oomox and redesigned devices icons

Sergei Eremenko (@SmartFinn) has contributed with our repository 25++ – has change this repository's directory structure to that of Papirus-like style, readpated Makefile and install.sh for making it officially compatible with @actionless's Oomox!

Yet else, @darcn181 has designed several alternatives of Sublime Text, session and user, giving you the freedom of choice for your taste! To see the list of alternative icons, you can check the instructions of how to change the alternative icons at the Github repository.

Finally, to be totally hybrid of Papirus, La Capitaine and Numix, and to be less traditional Suru++ Ubuntu, all devices icons have been redesigned.

In a week, I have increased from 4,7 thousand to 5,3 thousand icons and upgraded the file index.theme, adding new translations.

My next plans are to add gradient support for small icons of Suru++ icons, making compatible with Oomox and developing the CLI called Suru++ Colourise with the contributions of @actionless and @SmartFinn.
